# Employee Attrition Prediction using Machine Learning

## 📌 Project Overview
This project predicts whether an employee is likely to leave the company based on various factors such as job role, salary, work-life balance, business travel, and years at the company. The goal is to help HR teams identify employees at risk of leaving and take proactive retention measures.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze employee attrition data.
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Build and compare multiple machine learning models.
- Identify the key factors influencing employee attrition.
- Provide business recommendations for HR.

---

## 📂 Dataset
- **Dataset:** IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle
- **Rows:** 1470
- **Columns:** 35

## 💡 Key Findings
- Sales department has the highest attrition rate.
- Sales Representatives and Laboratory Technicians are more likely to leave.
- Overtime and frequent business travel increase attrition risk.
- Lower monthly income is associated with higher attrition.
- Employees are more likely to leave during their early years in the company.
